About CaliforniaShack

CaliforniaShack is your premier destination for vintage clothing resale and professional repair services. We specialize in authentic Y2K pieces, workwear, and fine jewelry, offering both restoration services and carefully curated selections.

Our expert team combines years of experience in vintage clothing restoration with a passion for preserving the quality and character of each piece. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, or restore, we're here to help maintain the legacy of vintage fashion.
